Glorious Mysteries

  1. The Resurrection — Christ rises from the dead, sanctifying hope.
  2. The Ascension — Christ ascends into Heaven, sanctifying longing.
  3. The Descent of the Holy Ghost — The Spirit descends, sanctifying wisdom.
  4. The Assumption of Mary — Mary is taken into Heaven, sanctifying purity.
  5. The Coronation of Mary — Mary is crowned Queen, sanctifying devotion.

“Sanctified be Thy name.” — Matthew 6:9, Rheims 1582

How to Pray the Rosary

  1. Make the Sign of the Cross
  2. Pray the Apostles’ Creed
  3. Pray one Our Father
  4. Pray three Hail Marys (for Faith, Hope, and Charity)
  5. Pray one Glory Be
  6. Announce the First Mystery and pray one Our Father
  7. Pray ten Hail Marys while meditating on the Mystery
  8. Pray one Glory Be (optional: Fatima Prayer)
  9. Repeat for all five Mysteries
  10. Conclude with the Hail Holy Queen and Final Prayer

“Sanctified be Thy name.” — Matthew 6:9, Rheims 1582
